Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Data Privacy & Local Processing

At MARK_Tools, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. Unlike traditional platforms, our tools are built on a Privacy-First, Client-Side Architecture. This means when you upload a PDF, compress an image, or format a JSON file, the processing happens locally within your web browser. We do not upload, store, view, or transmit your files to any external servers or databases. Once you close your browser tab, your data is gone.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies (Important)

MARK_Tools is a free service supported by advertising. We use Google AdSense to serve ads to our visitors. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.

  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our site's visitors based upon their visit to MARK_Tools and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.
  • We have no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

3. Log Files

Like many other Web sites, MARK_Tools makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. This information is automatically collected by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and CDN (Cloudflare) to analyze trends, administer the site, and track user movement. IP addresses are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

4. GDPR and CCPA Privacy Rights

We respect the rights provided under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Because MARK_Tools does not require account registration and processes user files locally, we do not store personal profiles. However, you have the right to:

  • Request disclosure of data collection practices regarding cookies.
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising via your Google Account settings.
  • Clear your browser's local storage and cache, which instantly wipes any temporary application data generated by our tools.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions. If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us via the contact page.
