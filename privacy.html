Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Data Privacy & Processing

MARK_Tools operates on a Privacy-First architecture. Most of our tools use client-side technology (JavaScript/WASM) which processes your data locally on your device. We do not store, view, or transmit your uploaded files (PDFs, Images, or Text) to our servers.

2. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use cookies (DART cookies) to show ads based on your visit to this and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

3. Log Files & Analytics

Like most websites, we collect standard log files (IP address, browser type, timestamp) via our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and Cloudflare for security and performance monitoring. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

4. CCPA & GDPR Rights

Our global users have the right to access, rectify, or erase any personal data (if any) we might hold. Since we do not use databases to store user information, your data footprint on our platform is virtually non-existent.
