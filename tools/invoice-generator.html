How to Create Professional, Privacy-First Invoices

As a freelancer, contractor, or small business owner, your invoice is often the final touchpoint you have with a client. Sending a messy Excel spreadsheet or a plain-text email demanding payment looks highly unprofessional and can delay your compensation. The MARK_Tools Invoice Generator empowers you to craft beautifully formatted, enterprise-grade PDF invoices in seconds.

Most commercial invoice generators require you to create an account and store your sensitive financial data (like bank account numbers, home addresses, and client names) in their cloud databases. We believe your financial data belongs strictly to you. By utilizing the jsPDF engine, our tool compiles your invoice entirely within your local browser.

Zero Server Data Retention

Data privacy is non-negotiable when handling billing information. Because MARK_Tools operates on a strict Client-Side Architecture, we do not have a database. We cannot see who you are billing, how much you are charging, or what your bank details are. Once you close the tab, the data vanishes.

Automated Tax & Math Logic

Making a calculation error on an invoice is embarrassing. Our integrated JavaScript engine automatically calculates item subotals, applies your custom tax rate (VAT, GST, etc.), factors in discounts, and produces a mathematically flawed grand total ready for export.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I save my invoice to edit it later?

Because we do not store your data on our servers for privacy reasons, there is no "Save Draft" feature tied to an account. However, your browser may temporarily remember input fields while the tab remains active. Always download your final PDF before closing the page.

Can I change the currency symbol?

Yes! You are not restricted to USD ($). You can manually input any international currency symbol (e.g., €, £, Rp) into the respective fields, and the PDF compiler will faithfully render it on the final document.
