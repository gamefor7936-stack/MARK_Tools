What is Case Converter & Word Counter?

Case Converter & Word Counter provides instant text transformation and detailed content analysis. Convert text between uppercase, lowercase, and title case while tracking word counts, character counts, and other writing metrics in real-time.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Enter Text: Type or paste your content in the text area.
  2. View Metrics: See live word and character counts.
  3. Convert Case: Click UPPERCASE or lowercase to transform the text.
  4. Copy Result: Select and copy the converted text.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does case conversion handle special characters?

Case conversion only affects letters (A-Z, a-z). Numbers, punctuation, and special characters remain unchanged.

What counts as a word in the word counter?

Words are counted by splitting on whitespace. Contractions and hyphenated words count as single words.

Does it support title case conversion?

Currently supports UPPERCASE and lowercase. For title case, consider using external tools or manual capitalization.
